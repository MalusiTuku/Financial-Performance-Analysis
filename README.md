# Financial Performance Analysis

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to provide an executive-level view of
financial performance by analysing revenue, profitability, cost
structure, and margin quality over time. The dashboard is designed to
support decision-making around profitability drivers, operating
efficiency, break-even performance, and seasonality effects.

---

## Key Business Questions Answered
- Is the business profitable overall, and why?
- Which categories and sub-categories drive gross profit?
- How efficient is the operating cost structure?
- What sales level is required to break even?
- How do seasonality and timing affect profitability?
- Which months represent best and worst financial performance?

---

## Key Metrics & Calculations
- Total Sales
- Gross Profit
- Net Profit
- Gross Margin %
- Net Margin %
- Operating Expenses % of Sales
- Break-even Sales Level
- Best / Worst Performing Month (Net Profit)
- Gross Profit by Category and Sub-Category

---

## Dashboard Pages Overview

### 1. Financial Performance Overview
An executive summary view highlighting overall revenue, profitability,
and financial health. Includes total sales, gross profit, net profit,
margin performance, and a cost breakdown showing the contribution of
COGS and operating expenses to overall profitability.

### 2. Profitability Drivers
A deep-dive analysis into margin quality and profit contribution by
product category and sub-category. This page highlights top and bottom
gross profit drivers, the relationship between sales volume and gross
margin, operating expense ratio, and the estimated break-even sales
level.

### 3. Cost Structure & Seasonality
An analysis of operating leverage and timing effects across months and
years. This page focuses on seasonality patterns, best and worst
performing months by net profit, operating expense behaviour over time,
and the relationship between total sales and operating expenses.
